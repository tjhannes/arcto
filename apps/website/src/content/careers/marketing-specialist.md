## Job Description

Automark is looking for a Marketing Specialist to join our marketing team and help drive awareness and pipeline for our AI-powered platform. You'll execute across channels — content, email, social, and events — with a bias for speed and experimentation.

## Job Responsibilities

**Execute multi-channel campaigns —** Plan and run campaigns across email, social media, paid ads, and content marketing.

**Create compelling content —** Write blog posts, case studies, landing pages, and email sequences that convert.

**Analyze and optimize —** Track performance metrics and continuously optimize campaigns based on data.

**Support product launches —** Help coordinate go-to-market launches for new features and releases.

## Qualifications

**3+ years of B2B marketing experience —** Track record of executing campaigns that drive measurable pipeline.

**Strong writer —** You can write clearly and compellingly across different formats and audiences.

**Data literacy —** Comfortable with marketing analytics tools and making decisions based on performance data.

## Bonus Points

- Experience marketing AI or automation products
- Familiarity with SEO and content strategy
- Experience with marketing automation tools (HubSpot, Mailchimp, etc.)

## Other Benefits

- Salary: $80K–$100K base + equity
- Hybrid — 3 days in California office
- Health, dental, and vision
- Quarterly team off-sites
