The holy grail of sales has always been: know who's ready to buy before they raise their hand. AI makes this possible by reading behavioral signals invisible to the human eye.

## What Is Buyer Intent?

Buyer intent is the real-time probability that a prospect is actively researching a solution and moving toward a purchase. Traditional methods use forms and demos to assess this. AI infers it from behavior.

## Signals AI Tracks

- Repeated visits to pricing pages
- Competitor comparison searches
- LinkedIn profile views from decision-makers
- Email engagement patterns
- Time spent on product documentation

### The Prediction Engine

AI models trained on thousands of historical purchases learn what patterns precede a conversion. When a new prospect matches those patterns, the system flags them with a confidence score.

> "Don't wait for leads to convert. Know who's converting before they do."

Teams using AI-powered intent signals close deals 30% faster because they focus energy where it matters most — at exactly the right moment.
